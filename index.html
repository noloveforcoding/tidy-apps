Privacy Policy

HadIt?  ·  Last updated: May 2025

The short version

HadIt? collects nothing. Stores everything on your device. Sends nothing anywhere. Ever.

Data Collection

HadIt? does not collect any personal data whatsoever. There are no analytics, no crash reporting tools, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind built into the app.

Data Storage

Everything you enter into HadIt? — item names, categories, notes, and photos — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage. Nothing is transmitted to any external server or third party.

Photos

Photos you add to items are stored on your device only. They are never uploaded, shared, or accessed by anyone other than you. HadIt? requests camera and photo library access solely to let you photograph your items — for no other purpose.

Third Parties

HadIt? contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or any external services. There are zero third-party dependencies in the app.

iCloud & Sync

HadIt? does not use iCloud, cloud sync, or any network service. Your data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

HadIt? does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and the date at the top. We will never introduce data collection without clearly communicating it here first.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at hello@tidy-apps.com
