Common questions

I deleted the app and lost my items. Can I get them back?

Unfortunately no. HadIt? stores everything locally on your device. When the app is deleted, the data goes with it. To protect your list, keep your iPhone backed up via iCloud or iTunes — this backs up your HadIt? data automatically.

I got a new phone. How do I transfer my items?

Restore your new iPhone from an iCloud or iTunes backup of your old phone. Your HadIt? data will be included in that backup and restored automatically.

Do I need to pay again on my new phone?

No. HadIt? is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID. Sign in with the same Apple ID on your new device, go to the App Store, and download HadIt? for free.

Can I use HadIt? on multiple devices?

Yes — download it on any device using the same Apple ID and it's free. Note that your items are stored per device and don't sync between devices yet. iCloud sync is on our roadmap.

Does HadIt? work without internet?

Yes, completely. HadIt? is fully offline. No internet connection is ever required.

Is my data private?

Completely. Nothing ever leaves your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

How do I delete an item?

Tap the item to open its detail view, then tap "Remove from list" at the bottom.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at hello@tidy-apps.com with a description of what happened. Screenshots help. We take bugs seriously and aim to fix them fast.
